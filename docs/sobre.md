# About Me

I am a researcher in **behavior economics**, **applied microeconomics** and **natural language processing**, with a focus on analyzing the impact of public policies with an economic perspective. I've been working with text datasets of lawcases, public finance, electoral and government database, observing the behavior according to specific topics.

I graduated with my PhD in Business Economics in 2023 and since I've been working with many topics in public policy, judicial behavior, behavior economics and data science. Across my research, the statistical framework and information extraction with natural language processing are the main topics in common.

Nowadays I'm in my post doctoral project at Insper, in the Center for Regulation and Democracy (CRD), which I'm also affiliated.
